WHAT IS
PRIVATE LABELING

Private labeling is a procedure used to manufacture bath and body products by a third party and sell them under your brand. You just need to worry about a few items during this procedure, like labeling design and your target market. We take care of the rest. We are a full turnkey solution, and we will provide product development, formulating, packaging procurement, and label printing, for a fully completed retail experience.

Minimum Order Quantities

We wanted to create a dedicated section for this as it is usually a common initial question. We have the lowest MOQ in the industry for a fully automated facility that manufactures to our standard. For products in a jar or a bottle, the MOQ is 500 units per variation. If we are creating custom pouches for you, which we can do in-house, the MOQ is 1000 units per variation. It is important to us to try and lower the barriers to entry to this market and our MOQ certainly helps us live up to that initiative.

Getting started

The experienced staff at The Midwest Sea Salt Company is ready to help you determine the best options and solutions for your professional skin care line. The following provides a basic outline of the steps we follow with each client.

  • Project Definition - in this phase you will interact with our Director of Business development - either in our showroom or by phone. In this meeting we will review your project, your brand, your time-line and your goals. Our focus is to ensure we understand your vision of what you are hoping to achieve for your customers and your business.
  • Scenting Options - While many of our products have a wonderfully natural scent from the organic and natural ingredients used, we understand the importance of offering different and beautiful scents for bath, body and some skin care products. We manufacture all our fragrances in house and have an array of 3500 fragrances to choose from. We offer fragrance oils and essential oils as well. We are sure to have something that tickles your fancy and all our fragrance oils are phthalate free fragrances.
  • Labeling Options - When it comes to labeling your product we only use the best, no matter the quantity. We use full color offset printing using water proof labels with lamination for every label. This is usually the first thing that your customers will see so we make sure you put your best foot forward. We do offer screen printing services as well. Your project specialists will work with you to provide you with the best option to meet your needs.
  • Product Selection - Based on your objectives and budget we will help you identify the best product selection for your skin care line. If we don’t currently offer what you are looking for, we usually can manufacture it for you.
  • Packaging Options - A key component to the success of your new line will be the packaging you choose. The only thing in the process that we may outsource is packaging. Being that every customer we work with has a different vision, we source our jars, bottles and tops from the largest distributor in the United States. We source a wide range of quality recyclable PET, glass and aluminum packaging choices for our clients. We also manufacture “in-house” custom printed flexible packaging, as well as custom printed tubes. We will present you with the best options available to meet your budget and branding goals.

FORMULATIONS

HOW DO I COME UP WITH A FORMULA?

We make this process super simple. Give us as many details as you have about the ingredients you would like in your product. We will take this info and fill in any missing ingredients or details to come up with your ingredient deck. So, you can have the entire ingredient deck or just an idea of the product with no ingredient details and we will assist with the formula.

CAN YOU USE MY FORMULATION?

Of course. If you have a formulation or ingredient deck, you can send this to us. We will confirm that we can recreate the formula and we will make it to spec.

CAN I MODIFY YOUR FORMULAS AND INGREDIENTS

Yes, you certainly can modify existing stock formulations of our retail products. Customizing your products will help provide a unique and individual branding opportunity since a unique product will help you stand out from the competition.

What if I don’t see a product I am looking for?

We absolutely can formulate and manufacture products other than those listed. We have accumulated an extensive library of formulations consisting of thousands of products. If you don’t see what you’re looking for, please contact us, as we most likely can create a product formulation to suit your needs.

Can you replicate an established product?

In most cases, we can. In this instance, we would ask that you send us a sample. Our formulators will assess our ability to recreate the product and will let you know if we think the project is a good fit for our abilities.

Can you create natural or organic products?

Yes. Every project that we work on is custom to your needs. If you need us to use natural or organic ingredients, we can certainly accommodate this.

Do you test on animals?

Never. We are a 100% cruelty-free facility.

Where can I find the ingredients?

We do not list our ingredients on our pricelist for a reason. We create each ingredient deck based on your needs. You are not tied to using a specific formula. Tell us what you need, and we will create an ingredient deck around your needs.

Can you provide COA and MSDS sheets?

Yes, we can give you MSDS sheets and COAs for every product that we make for you.

SAMPLES

Can I order samples?

Yes, you can order samples. As a company, we do not have formulation fees. So, if we think we can make a product we do not charge you for formulating the product to assess our ability to make it for you. We do charge a sampling fee once we have assessed that we can make the product and are ready to send it to you. Our sampling fee starts at $25 and will depend on the product and quantity needed. Inquire about samples to see the cost of your specific needs. The sampling fee does include shipping as well.

Will my samples come in full-size packaging?

No, samples are merely for testing the formula. Because we are a custom facility, we do not order packaging until we have a finalized order. We can always request packaging from vendors for samples if you need an actual sample of the packaging but in general, the samples will not come in retail packaging.

I need to send containers, labels, or samples. How do I do that?

Please make sure your vendor marks the box with YOUR name on it. We receive an enormous amount of stock packaging through our facilities, so it is hard to determine one customer's packaging from another. PACKAGING

What kind of packaging do you offer?

Packaging is the only part of the private label process that we do not manufacture in-house. Because we want your product to be based on your vision, we don’t limit products to being put in “stock” packaging. We source all the packaging for each product. If we can find it available in the market, we can fill it. We also can use the packaging that you send to us as well. So, there are no limits on the packaging.

Can I use my packaging?

Absolutely. If you have packaging you would like to send or a preferred supplier you can always send us packaging, components, or ingredients. We always say we will do as much or as little as you need us to. So, you can send any components you would like to complete your product.

Can you create a custom pouch for me?

Yes, it’s one of our specialties. We manufacture custom-printed stand-up pouches here in-house. The MOQ for custom pouches is 1000 units and these pouches can accommodate dry products as well as liquid pouches that include a spout for dispensing. Custom pouches are a great way to lower costs while also presenting amazing branding on custom packaging. They also provide for endless size customizations because we build the pouch around the product, we are putting in it.

Can you make a custom retail box for my product?

Yes, we can make and print custom retail boxes in-house to be included in your final retail packaging.

DESIGN & LABELS

We currently do not offer label design. Our expertise is in the actual creation of an amazing formula and end-user retail experience. Will I get a label template?

We will supply you with the label template for any product we make. You can then take this template and either design the label yourself or give this to your designer. The template will let you know where our equipment will be cut so we can make sure that none of your critical images or wording are cut in the label creation process.

LOGISTICS & SHIPPING

How much does shipping usually cost?

Shipping costs will be different depending on where you are shipping the items to. Let us know your proposed order and address and we can get you a shipping quote. You may also always use your own shipping company to pick up your products from our facility as well.

Yes, we can. For a frame of reference, we ship roughly 10-15 truckloads of private-label products to Amazon FBA daily. We do not charge shipping fees for products going to Amazon FBA. Amazon will give you labels to have us apply to your shipment, and we will ship them out accordingly.

What are your turnaround times?

This will certainly depend on your specific needs and the season but in general products that are in a jar or a bottle will take 15-20 business days and products in custom pouches will take 4-6 weeks to complete… start to finish.

Do you offer drop shipping?

No, we currently do not offer this service. Being that we are an automated high-volume facility we are not set up to handle individual drop shipping orders at this time.
